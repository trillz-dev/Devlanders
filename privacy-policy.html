TMS (Registered as Touch Me Soft d.o.o. in Belgrade, Serbia, tax identification no. 108706008), is committed to respecting and protecting your privacy and strives to keep your personal information secure. This policy covers the collection and use of information obtained through the website on which this notice appears. If you have questions or complaints regarding our privacy notice or practices, please contact us via one of the methods provided below.

Personal Information Collected

TMS collects personal information through this website in order to communicate with current and potential clients and business partners.

Personal information we may collect include:

  • Name
  • E-mail address
  • Company name
  • Other information you voluntarily send through this website

On this website we collect personal information in two ways:

  • When you send us a message through contact form
  • When you leave a comment in our blog section

Use of Personal Information

When we collect your personal information via the contact form we will use it only for the purpose of direct contact with you, so we can answer your questions and inquiries in the best possible way.

When we collect your personal information via the comments section in our blog, it used to enable better management of the comments section, and to display some of the information publicly in the comments section. We will not use it for direct private communication unless you specifically ask for this type of communication in a message sent as a comment.

Cookies and Other Technologies

A cookie is a small text file that is stored on a user’s computer for record-keeping purposes.

Analytics / Log Files

Our website uses services which gather certain information automatically and store it in log files. This information includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, internet service provider (ISP), referring pages, landing pages, exit pages, operating system, date/time stamp, and clickstream data. This information is used to administer and improve website performance, to diagnose server problems, to ensure the security of our website, and to optimize our marketing efforts. These services do not provide us with access to any of your personal information.

3rd Party Tracking

We use third party cookies, web beacons, images and scripts from our partners and service providers to help us better manage content on our website and to manage our marketing efforts. These do not provide us with access to any of your personal information.

Social Media

Our website includes social media features, such as the Facebook Like button, social sharing buttons and similar. These features may collect information such as your IP address, which page you are visiting on our website, and may set a cookie to function properly. Social media features and widgets are either hosted by a third party or hosted directly on our website. Your interactions with these features are governed by the privacy policy of the company providing such feature.

Access to Information

Whenever we collect your personal information, we take steps to help ensure that it is accurate, complete, and up-to-date. If your personal information changes, or if you no longer want to communicate with us, you may ask us to find out which information we have about you, and to correct, update, amend, or delete any or all information by contacting us via one of the methods listed below.
